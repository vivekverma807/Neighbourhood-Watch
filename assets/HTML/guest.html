Welcome to Neighbourhood Watch

Neighbourhood Watch is your local safety companion. Our platform empowers residents to stay informed about community incidents, report suspicious behavior, and communicate with each other in real time. Together, we build a safer and stronger neighborhood.

  • Get Started

Real-Time Incident Reporting

Instantly report crimes, suspicious activities, or emergencies. Our streamlined reporting system ensures your voice reaches the right people at the right time.

Active Community Participation

Our platform encourages residents to stay engaged. Whether it's neighborhood discussions, event planning, or alerts—community input drives safer living.

Emergency Notification System

Receive immediate alerts about critical threats such as fires, thefts, or missing persons. Stay informed and take action when seconds matter.

Collaborative Safety Approach

When neighbors unite, safety becomes stronger. Connect with local security volunteers, police liaisons, and fellow residents to share knowledge and support.

Voices from the Community

Awareness Drives

Participate in local awareness events about safety, self-defense, and emergency preparedness. Knowledge is power—and it saves lives.

Smart Neighborhood Mapping

Visualize crime-prone zones and recent reports using our dynamic map feature. Navigate your area with better awareness and avoid hotspots.

Volunteer Network

Join hands with volunteers and emergency responders who dedicate time to keeping our locality peaceful and crime-free.
